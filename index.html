What I do

My life has been an intersting collection of different studies, work and hobbies. Listed below are some major examples.

Study

In 2016 I completed my VCE at St. Leonard's College. I then studied a Bachelor of Science in 2017 and 2018 before transferring to a Bachelor of IT in 2019.

Work

I worked at the Dingley Tennis Club from 2014-2016. In 2017 I started working as a Food and Beverage attendant in the Dingley Hotel. This is just a fancy way of saying I am a waiter and bartender. I am still working at the Dingley Hotel.

Acting

I have also done a fair amount of acting work. I have performed on stage numerous times with the Hart Theatre Company and I have appeared in several TV commercials and TV programs. I have even been fortunate enough to appear in some feature films such as Holding the Man and Predestination.

Dungeons And Dragons
